Down, Beckenham, Kent.  Dec 29th 1880    My dear Dr. Burdon Sanderson    If you would not object to sign the enclosed certificate for my son Francis, it would greatly please him & me. But you must not think of doing it if you doubt about the propriety of his candidateship.  I enclose an awful envelope   and remain     Yours very sincerely  C.L. Darwin    Please enclose a slip of paper with the date of your lecture at R. Institution. CD
